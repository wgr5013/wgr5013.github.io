What is PepeDapp?

No matter what you are feeling, there’s a Pepe for that. That’s the beauty of the Pepe. It’s the beauty of expression. Pepedapp is a game where users can create, buy and sell original rare pepe artwork.

Create and Submit Original Artwork

Users can submit their artwork to PepeDapp to be featured and sold on the marketplace. Creators get 96% of the sale of their original Pepe Cards.

Learn More »

Buy

Buy original Pepe Cards directly in our marketplace using Metamask. The price of the card is determined by the seller. By default, Metamask will open to the lowest asking price of that card. Once owned, your cards will appear in your "my pepes" wallet.

Learn More »

Sell

Once a Pepe Card is owned (either via submission or buy purchasing a card) users can set the sell price and post it up for sale in the marketplace
