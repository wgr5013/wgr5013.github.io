    • Creating & Submitting

  • How do I submit my art?
    Users can create original digital Rare Pepe artwork and post a 600x600 JPG of their work here.
  • What happens once my artwork is submitted?
    Once your Rare Pepe artwork is submitted, the team at Rare Pepe will review it. If it meets all of our requirements, we will put it on the pipeline for release at your sell price during our first issuance of user generated artwork.

    We are working on a way to automate and decentralize this process for the future.
